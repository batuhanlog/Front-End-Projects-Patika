About Us

As Hobby World, we inspire passionate and curious people to discover, develop and share their hobbies. We believe in the importance of adding color to ordinary moments and supporting people on their personal development journey.

We offer a wide range of hobbies for all ages and interests with our passionate team members, each of whom are experts in their field. Together with us, we ensure that you have an enjoyable experience while taking up a new hobby, exploring existing hobbies in more depth or sharing your hobbies with others.
